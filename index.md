 StudyPulse – Smart Study Tracker

Project Description

StudyPulse is a mobile productivity application designed to help students improve their study habits through structured time tracking and goal setting.

The application enables students to monitor their daily study hours, manage different academic subjects, and set achievable study goals. By providing progress tracking and motivational support, StudyPulse encourages consistency, discipline, and effective time management.

Students can log study sessions, view their progress over time, and stay inspired through motivational quotes integrated into the system.


Objectives

- Track study hours
- Set academic goals
- Manage subjects
- Improve time management
- Encourage consistency
